# Law / Regulation Source Digest v0.1

## GENIUS Act and U.S. implementation materials
The archive contains the GENIUS Act public-law text and several implementation-related materials, including OCC NPRM, Treasury state-level regime principles, and FinCEN/Treasury AML-CFT materials. These sources will support the U.S. regulatory chapter, especially issuer eligibility, redemption, reserve standards, AML/CFT obligations, and federal-state allocation of authority. The next extraction step should separate public-law text from proposed rules and interpretive/implementation materials.

## CLARITY Act
The archive contains the CLARITY Act reported bill and House Report. These should be treated as digital-asset market-structure sources rather than purely stablecoin-specific sources. They are useful for defining the broader legal perimeter of digital assets, intermediaries, and market activities.

## MiCA / EBA
The archive includes EBA/ESA consumer/factsheet material, EBA final reporting ITS for ART/EMT reporting under MiCAR, and an EBA opinion on PSD2-MiCA interplay. These are useful for EU regulatory implementation, but the official MiCA Regulation text still needs to be fetched or manually downloaded.

## BoE / UK
The archive includes BoE Financial Stability Report and FPC Record materials. These support financial-stability framing, but the specific systemic stablecoin consultation remains in the external fetch queue.

## NYDFS
NYDFS guidance is not present as a standalone source in the archive, even though RLUSD and GUSD reserve reports rely on NYDFS stablecoin guidance criteria. It remains high-priority for external fetch/manual download.
