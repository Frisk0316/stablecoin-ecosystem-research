# Failure Case Source Digest v0.1

Failure-case source coverage remains incomplete in v0.1. The archive includes issuer documents for Maker/Sky and Tether, but not dedicated event-analysis sources for Terra UST, Iron Finance, USDC SVB depeg, USDT reserve controversy, or DAI/USDS collateral stress episodes.

This module should be handled after the issuer, law, central bank, and payment-settlement modules are stable, because the failure-case chapter depends on the taxonomy of stablecoin types and reserve/redemption mechanisms.
