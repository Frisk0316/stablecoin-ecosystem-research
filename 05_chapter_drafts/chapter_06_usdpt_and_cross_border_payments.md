# Chapter 6 — USDPT / UDSPT and Cross-border Payments

Working thesis: USDPT/UDSPT should be analysed as a potential change to payment and settlement architecture, not simply as a generic stablecoin.

Required distinctions:

- SWIFT is primarily a financial messaging network, not the final settlement asset.
- Correspondent banking provides relationships, accounts, compliance and settlement pathways.
- Fedwire/CHIPS provide wholesale settlement/clearing functions in USD.
- Western Union has a consumer-facing and agent/corridor network that must be analysed separately.

Open question:

- Does USDPT/UDSPT replace customer-facing remittance rails, agent settlement, treasury liquidity management, or only part of the back-end settlement layer?
