# Chapter 6 - USDPT / UDSPT and Cross-border Payments

Working thesis: USDPT/UDSPT should be analyzed as a potential change to
payment and settlement architecture, not simply as a generic stablecoin.

## Evidence Now Available

- Anchorage's comment letter remains useful background: Anchorage Digital Bank
  described itself as issuer of USAT, USDGO, and USDtb, and said it expected to
  begin issuing UDSPT with Western Union as brand partner (`CLAIM_023`).
- Western Union's October 28, 2025 announcement states that USDPT would be
  built on Solana, issued by Anchorage Digital Bank, and connected to a Digital
  Asset Network (`CLAIM_051`).
- Western Union's March 31, 2026 launch release states that USDPT is a
  USD-denominated payment stablecoin, fully backed by U.S. dollars, issued by
  Anchorage Digital Bank N.A., and built on Solana (`CLAIM_052`).
- Fireblocks' infrastructure material adds operational clues: agent settlement,
  wallet infrastructure, compliance tooling, TRES accounting support, and
  MT940/MT942 translation are presented as part of the USDPT ecosystem
  (`CLAIM_053`).

## Required Distinctions

- SWIFT is primarily a financial messaging network, not the final settlement
  asset.
- Correspondent banking provides relationships, accounts, compliance, liquidity
  management, and settlement pathways.
- Fedwire/CHIPS provide wholesale USD settlement and clearing functions.
- Western Union has a consumer-facing remittance network and an agent/corridor
  network that must be analyzed separately from bank-to-bank rails.

## Current Interpretation

The evidence supports product existence, issuer identity, blockchain choice,
and an infrastructure partnership frame. It does not yet prove that USDPT
replaces SWIFT, correspondent banking, Fedwire, CHIPS, or consumer-facing
remittance rails. The safer interpretation is that USDPT may change some
back-end liquidity, treasury, agent-settlement, or wallet workflows, but the
exact workflow still needs primary product documents.

## Open Questions

- Confirm whether the durable product name is USDPT or UDSPT across all
  primary materials.
- Obtain product terms, reserve reports, contract addresses, direct redemption
  rights, and user/agent workflow documentation.
- Determine whether Western Union agents handle retail stablecoin cash-out,
  only fiat endpoints, or only internal/agent settlement.
