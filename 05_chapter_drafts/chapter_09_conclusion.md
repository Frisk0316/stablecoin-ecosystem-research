# Chapter 9 — Conclusion

Draft conclusion direction:

Stablecoins should be understood as a reconfiguration of dollar liquidity, payment networks, reserve asset demand, and regulatory perimeter rather than as a clean replacement for banks or central bank money.

The final report should distinguish:

- What stablecoins improve: programmability, 24/7 transferability, global wallet-level access, some settlement speed.
- What stablecoins do not solve by themselves: finality in central bank money, AML/CFT at system level, bank deposit substitution risks, reserve liquidation risk, legal redemption rights for all holders.
