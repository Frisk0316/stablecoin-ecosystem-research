# Chapter 1 — Stablecoins as an On-chain Extension of the Dollar System

Working thesis: major fiat-backed stablecoins are not separate from the dollar system. They wrap bank deposits, Treasury bills, repo, money market funds, custodians, and regulated financial institutions into blockchain-transferable claims.

Key evidence to develop:

- BIS: stablecoins fall short as the backbone of money under singleness, elasticity, integrity.
- ECB/BIS: stablecoins create safe-asset-demand channels through reserve holdings.
- Issuer reserve reports: reserves are heavily tied to cash, deposits, T-bills, repo or MMF-like instruments.
